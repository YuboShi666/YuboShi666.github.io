    Research interests

    Nankai

    During my PhD at Nankai University, I study topology and Non-Hermitian physics. Including

    1. characterizing high-order topological phase dynamically;
    2. Majorana edge modes in non-Hermitian Kitaev chains;
    3. Exotic quantum states in spin systems;

    PKS

    During my visit to PKS, I have studied some topics in quantum physics and biophysics, including lattice gauge theory; quantum circuits; active matter; etc.

    Future

    In the future, besides focusing on my current research area, I also want to learn more about machine learning, programmable metamaterials, optics, and so on. Moreover, it is exciting to learn some technical details behind experiments.
    Learning itself is a very enjoyable journey ;).

    Numerical methods
